Our multi-purpose Event Center

Iconic Castle Event Center is a social event centre located in an high-brow area of Oshodi-Isolo LGA of Lagos-State. It is on a major road that is very accessible from most parts of Lagos mainland. The facility is specifically designed to host wedding parties; birthday parties; anniversary; celebration of life; corporate events like christmas and end of the year party,religious activities; exhibitions; shows; award ceremonies and corporate meetings and training. We have a dedicated team of Facility Managers and Customer Care Assistants who are seasoned in event hosting, and are available to assist you at every stage of your planning and execution.
